Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Is there a fee to join as a Baker Partner?

Absolutely not. Joining Baqers Canada as a baker partner is completely free. We earn when you earn — our model is commission-based, so there's zero upfront cost or monthly fee.

What areas do you cover?

We're currently active in Toronto and the East GTA region, including Scarborough, Markham, Pickering, Ajax, Whitby, and Oshawa. We're expanding regularly.

Do I need to be a registered business?

Not initially. Many of our partners start as home bakers. Our free legal cheat sheet in the Starter Kit will walk you through Ontario's requirements and help you grow your setup properly.

How does delivery work?

You prepare the order at your location. Baqers Canada coordinates pickup and same-day delivery to the customer through our logistics partners. You don't need to deliver anything yourself.

When and how do I get paid?

Baker partners receive weekly payouts via e-transfer or direct deposit. You'll have a clear dashboard showing all earnings, pending orders, and payment history.
